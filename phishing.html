Understanding Phishing Attacks

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers pose as legitimate institutions to trick individuals into providing sensitive data like usernames, passwords, and credit card details.

Common Phishing Signs

  • ⚠️ Urgent or threatening language
  • ⚠️ Generic greetings ("Dear Customer")
  • ⚠️ Suspicious email addresses
  • ⚠️ Misspellings and bad grammar
  • ⚠️ Suspicious attachments or links
  • ⚠️ Requests for personal information

How to Protect Yourself

Think Before You Click

Hover over links to see the actual URL before clicking.
